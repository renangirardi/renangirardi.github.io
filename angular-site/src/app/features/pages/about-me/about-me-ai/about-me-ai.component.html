GenAI enthusiast

My curiosity for new technologies led me, at the end of 2022, to start experimenting with Generative AI tools, beginning with ChatGPT. Since then, this universe has become part of my daily life, transforming the way I study, work, and create. Over time, I’ve explored tools such as Gemini, Claude, GitHub Copilot, Windsurf, Stable Diffusion, Midjourney, and DALL·E. Each of them has shown me new ways to boost productivity, accelerate learning, and bring creative ideas to life.

But my relationship with GenAI goes beyond practical use. It has also been a space to play and have fun. I created the Instagram account @ai.cthulhu, a project with no professional intent, just a way to share funny images I make together with friends. In these creations, Cthulhu, the classic monster from H. P. Lovecraft’s literature and an iconic figure in board games like Eldritch Horror, which we often played, shows up in unlikely situations: grocery shopping, starring in famous movies, or living everyday moments.
